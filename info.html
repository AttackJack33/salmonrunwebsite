Who am I?

My name is Jack K and I am currently taking a Web Design course through high school. Though this isn't an assignment of some sorts, it is a personal project


Why did I make this?

I am what one might say, an enthusiast, about Splatoon™. Ever since I've seen the original trailers for 1 and 2, I've wanted to play it for so long. When I got my first switch back in 2017, I got Splatoon 2 and played through it like it was nothing. It wasn't until the Action Vs. Comedy Splatfest when I started playing online. Once I did, I invested a lot of time into Salmon Run, mastering it's mechanics and how it works. It wasn't until I found Hazmy's channel when I found out that there was a whole community that was dedicated to finding data for everyone, specifically the Salmon Run Reports. It wasn't until his discord got raided and decided to stop doing Salmon Run Reports. So I decided to take it into my own hands. With the info gathered from the reports and the data of each weapon uploaded on Inkpedia, I managed to create this website for everyone to look into and learn more about Salmon Run.
